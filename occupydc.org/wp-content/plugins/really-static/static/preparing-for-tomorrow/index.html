Preparing for Tomorrow!

September 30th, 2011 · Bob · News 5 comments

I would like to thank everyone that has contributed to this cause and all the energy that has put in. I would just like to remind you guys that you should spend some time today preparing for tomorrow. Make sure you have everything you need including food, water, cellphone, blankets, sweater, raincoat, signs, flyers, etc! Make sure to get familiar with the DC laws and go over our list that can be found here.

Currently the weather predictions don’t look all the good for us and it will be rainy and cold so bring hot chocolate, a raincoat, and warm blankets and sweaters. There shouldn’t be any Metro delays but please do keep in mind that the Metro’s schedule is a tad slower in the weekend. You can go to the McPherson Square by either the Red line (Farragut North) or Orange and Blue (McPherson Sq). For directions and a map please have a look here on Google Maps.

If you can’t spend the entire day at McPherson Sq please just come by at some point of the day. We can use all the support that’s out there. For those that can not make it don’t worry, we will keep you updated on the website, Facebook, and the Twitter feed. If you can’t make it but like to help than you can do so by spreading the word out by telling your friends and sharing this website online.
