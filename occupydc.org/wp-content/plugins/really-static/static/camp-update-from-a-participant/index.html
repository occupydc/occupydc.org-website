Camp update from a participant

October 1st, 2011 · Chris · Other 2 comments

“I am speechless about all of us who’ve been involved in this effort in the past 5 days whether with advice via email, support at the camp, planning, outreach, etc. There is so much synergy among those at the camp today it’s hard to describe. Match.com couldn’t have brought together a more compatible set of minds. Everyone I’ve spoken to is so smart and down to earth and understands the situation we and the country are in at number of different levels which allows them to be both pragmatic as well as idealistic. I have a renewed sense hope in this country because of the people I’ve met down at the square today. Thank you all and much love!”

As of later today/tomorrow we will start posting meetings from the General Assemblies. We’ll try to aggregate as much media content as we can on this site as well to give you all a glimpse of what people are blogging about, taking pictures and videos of, etc.
