Ongoing planning

September 28th, 2011 · Chris · Other 7 comments

Tonight at 10PM we are have another discussion planning this Saturday’s events. There’s a new tab called Meetings (http://occupydc.org/wp-content/plugins/really-static/static//wp-content/plugins/really-static/static/meetings/) where we’ll list the agendas and minutes for each meeting. The agenda for tonight here at this location: http://occupydc.org/wp-content/plugins/really-static/static//wp-content/plugins/really-static/static/agenda-0928/ please comment to make additions to it. The comments will be moved to the page itself up until the meeting starts. After the meeting one of us will put up the notes of what was discussed, what we agreed or disagreed upon and what might be coming up for discussion the next day.

You can join the meetings each night at 10PM EST on ircnode channel #occupydc or by clicking on this link: http://irc.lc/ircnode/occupydc (web client for the irc channel).
Check out this page: http://occupydc.org/wp-content/plugins/really-static/static//wp-content/plugins/really-static/static/chatting-and-conferencing-on-irc/ for instructions on the chat, how to secure a username, etc.
