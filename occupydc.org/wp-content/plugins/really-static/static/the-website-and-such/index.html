The website and such

September 30th, 2011 · Bob · Website 15 comments

Please donate to the website: http://widget.chipin.com/widget/id/a4edf45f3ac8eeb3

I have been put in charge of the website and will be making sure it is up and running! At the moment we’re using WordPress.com to host our website, but we are planning to move soon to our own servers, hopefully next week. Until then we cannot do any big changes or make it more user-friendly than it already is. I would like to get the community’s input on what we can add, change, or remove as this is a people’s movement.

The goal of the website is to organize our ideas, goals, meetings, times and dates allowing people to quickly check what is happening via computer and smartphones. The website is here to bring people together and to act as a main hub for our Twitter, Facebook, and Tumblr page(s).

Please feel free to comment with any ideas, suggestions, or comments!
