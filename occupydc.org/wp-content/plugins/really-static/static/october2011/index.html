October 2011

September 25th, 2011 · Chris · 2 comments

Stop the Machine is a separate nation-wide movement in solidarity with #OccupyWallSt. We plan on joining them when they come to DC on October 6th.

They seem to have a lot of institutions and organizations behind them and we’re excited that the date of their protests is so close in proximity to #OccupyWallStreet. This means a lot more momentum for the movement to end the corporatocracy.

Their website is http://october2011.org
