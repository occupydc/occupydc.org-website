Solidarity protests starting Oct 1st

September 25th, 2011 · Chris · News 16 comments

Thank you for the participation and to all of you who have voted. After looking at the results and a conference tonight (please join every evening at 10PM EST using the chat link under contact) most were in favor for participating in McPherson Square or moving there after Saturday. As a consequence McPherson was picked as the start and camp location.

Additional pros for McPherson:
  • McPherson Sq. is on K St. – lobby HQ
  • Title 36 (Parks, Forests, and Public Property) lists the square as one of the 4 parks not needing a permit for protests of 500 people or less
  • Steps from metro lines (blue & orange) and buses
  • Plenty of traffic
  • Locations with WiFi all around
  • Benches and shade from trees
  • Not close to outposts of the police or Secret Service

A few concerns about Freedom Plaza:
  • Easy for police to corner people there .. not many exit opportunities
  • Slab of stone vs soil
  • No shade (those with laptops can appreciate)

A few concerns about Pershing Park:
  • Way too close for comfort to the Secret Service
  • History of police violence in the area
