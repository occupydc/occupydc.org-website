F.A.Q.

September 30th, 2011 · Bob · 4 comments

To get your questions answered ASAP visit our Tumblr Page: occupywashdc.tumblr.com – OR comment below!

When and where?
Every day @ McPherson Square. Right near K St. Metro accessible on the Red, Orange and Blue lines. Bring food, laptops, supplies, your friends, your brains, and some motivation!

What’s going on exactly?
This will be our inaugural General Assembly. Everyone attending will be able to speak their minds as to how they feel OccupyDC should be set up, ideas, conversations, etc. Remember that OccupyWallStreet took months to plan. We are sort of winging it so it’s important to have this general meeting to see how many people are interested in this movement and where everyone stands!

Why?
Millions of Americans are living in poverty. Income disparity between the rich and the poor are the largest in over 30 years. 20+ million are unemployed. Corporations are raking in RECORD profits. Money is corrupting our politics. The real questions is WHY NOT? We want to stand in total solidarity with OccupyWallStreet and the 50+ other Occupy movements in this country and around the world.

How long do you plan on staying in McPherson Square?
The tentative plan is to occupy for several months, like OccupyWallStreet. Of course, many things can change. It all depends on what the group wants or decides. This is a NON-VIOLENT, PEACEFUL protest!

Who can I contact for more info?
occupydc@hush.com or go to our website.

I’m confused! Why do I keep seeing Oct. 6th as the official date of occupation?
October 6th is the date that Stop the Machine/October2011 is coming to Freedom Plaza to protest anti-militarism and other things. They just happened to take the# occupydc name before us but they are a separate, nationwide organization that are making their voices heard. They have been planning for this date for months in advance and are very well organized. OccupyDC is a local movement, completely grassroots that is directly connected to the OccupyTogether and OccupyWallStreet movement. We do, however, plan on joining Stop the Machine when they come to town. Again, they are a separate group from us that just happened to have good timing!

How can I help?
SPREAD THE WORD about us! Follow our Tumblr, Twitter, Facebook, etc! Visit our website. Email us. Come to our meeting on Oct. 1st. There is a lot you can do, nothing is too small!
