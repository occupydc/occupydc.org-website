Update from McPherson Square

October 1st, 2011 · Bob · Status Update 4 comments

I was at McPherson Square between 12pm and 5:15pm (had to leave the General Assembly) but would like to tell the world that everything is going great. Although we had a rainy and windy cold day we have seen people come and go and some media attention. We had tons of art supplies, made dozens of signs, had pizza, coffee, bread, and lots of interesting and dedicated people.Â  When I left at 5:15 I roughly counted between 115 and 135 people at the General Assembly, but we definitely had a lot more people over the span of the entire day. Even if you just stayed for half an hour I would like to thank you so much for stopping by. There will be a post later regarding what we agreed on and went over in the two General Assemblies we had today.

You can find photos on our Flickr which will be updated by the hour as more photos and videos are getting posted online.
