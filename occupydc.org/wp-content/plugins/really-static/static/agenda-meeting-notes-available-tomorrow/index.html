Agenda & meeting notes available tomorrow

September 30th, 2011 · Chris · Other No comments

Tonight’s been a long night already for all of us and we’ve accomplished a lot. Tomorrow is our last day to organize before the first GA on Saturday.

We’ll put up an October 1st preparedness page with information on legal tips, what to bring, ground rules, etc. That will be available before EOD Friday. Before then we need as many people as possible to familiarize themselves with the General Assembly rules: http://takethesquare.net/2011/07/31/quick-guide-on-group-dynamics-in-peoples-assemblies

You’ll see that a number of generic tabs have disappeared. Today we’ll be populating a couple of pages with pertinent information about the event. We’re all now making a move from the planning stage to an operational stage and the site will start reflecting that.
