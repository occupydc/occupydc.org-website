Post-meeting 09/28

September 29th, 2011 · Chris · Other 10 comments

Meeting notes are up here.

General conclusions:

- despite having other issues on the agenda our main message is to repeal corporate personhood and in particular Citizens United

- our group will continue to remain a democratically organized group without leaders but with plenty of coordinators who maintain the necessary functions of the movement

- although we are in agreeance with all the other occupy groups we plan on having the above-mentioned focus as a goal

- we do not expect chats prior to Oct 1st to help us with refining a message or really achieve anything past planning and logistics

- we understand there will be disagreement and are ready to unite around those issues on which we all agree
